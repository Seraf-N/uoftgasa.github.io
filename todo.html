New Student To Do List

  • Arrive in Toronto before Labour Day (first Monday in September) to be in time for orientation

  • Apply for your health insurance plan

    • If you are coming from another Canadian province, register for OHIP at a Service Ontario
    • If you are coming from another country, register for UHIP online

  • Get your TCard.

  • Activate your UTORid.

  • Set up your wifi.

  • [Log in to ACORN].(https://acorn.utoronto.ca)

    • Set up direct deposit
    • Defer your tuition payment (see the Funding Package section for more details)
    • Enrol in AST1501Y and additional optional courses (see Courses) for more details.

  • Set up HRIS Payroll profile

    • If you are an international student, you will need to get a social insurance number at a Service Canada location

  • Activate your ‘astro’ mail with Hugh Zhao

    • Recommended: set up forwarding of this email to another address

  • Access the internal section of the DAA website - the password will be in your welcome packet.

  • Find your assigned desk from Margaret Meaney.

  • Get your keys.

    • If your desk is in AB you should get the following from Lillian Lanca:
      • AB building key,
      • AB mailroom key,
      • AB office key.
    • If your desk is in MP, you should get the following:
      • MP building key from Jenny Jung,
      • MP office key from Jenny Jung,
      • AB mailroom key from Lillian Lanca,
      • AB building key from Lillian Lanca.

  • Get your printer/copier codes from Lillian Lanca

  • Get the library door code from librarian Lee Robbins
